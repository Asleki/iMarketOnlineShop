Orders & Delivery

Once your order is shipped, we will send you an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can also view the status of your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'My Orders' section.

Delivery times and costs vary depending on the seller, product, and your location. The estimated delivery time and shipping fee will be displayed on the product page and at checkout before you finalize your purchase.

If your order has not yet been shipped, it may be possible to change the address. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible with your order number and the new address.

Payments & Returns

We accept a variety of secure payment methods, including M-Pesa, Visa, and MasterCard.

Our return policy allows you to return most products within a certain number of days after delivery for a refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition. Please see our Terms & Conditions for a detailed policy.

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days after the returned item is received and inspected. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method.
